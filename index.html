SRC -SAE RTU CHAPTER

The "automative club "of our college .It is a group of young and enthusiastic brains who are passionate to learn about design, manufacturing and operation of automobiles . With the help of very supportive seniors and under teachers guidance members of the club backed respectable position in various championships . Projects like eco- kart ,go- kart ,rc nitro and many more thrilling work ignite the spirit in each and every student to work and learn consistently .

our teams

AUTOMATE 1.0

Automate 1.0 was the first ecokart team of RTU Kota . Team with it's hardwork and compassion bagged great position in various karting events. Team with it's practical knowledge inspired many other students to join and participate in various other events

AUTOMATE 2.0

Automate 2.0 is the second version of team automate . Teams achievement at Eco kart series and nekc was exceptionally well. Members worked with consistency and dillegence and with it's achievements played a very important role in bringing SRC among the top Technical Club of RTU Kota

EXPENDABLES

Going with the name ,This team expanded itself to various events like ECO KART series , NEKC and many more . Not limiting itself to karts , team expandables enthusiastically performed in Robo war events .

ANIGAR

Anigar is the first go-kart team of RTU Kota . Team comprises of first year young brains who with their hard work , persistence and team spirit participated in THAR ( Techno - Management Fest of RTU Kota )
